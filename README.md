# 🏮 Lantern of Nabatea

> *Unveiling the Secrets of Ancient AlUla*

A story-driven action-adventure game set in the ancient **Nabataean era** of AlUla, Saudi Arabia. Play as **Oday**, a young man descending into forbidden hand-carved caves to find his missing brother and father — confronting jinn drawn from traditional Arabian folklore along the way.

Built as a final project for the **Game Design program at Tuwaiq Academy**.

## 🗺️ The World

Most adventure games pull from Greek or Norse mythology. *Lantern of Nabatea* steps somewhere far less explored: the legends of the Arabian Peninsula. The setting is the Nabataean era — a world of legendary trade routes and monumental architecture carved straight into desert stone.

The journey takes you from a safe oasis into the depths of the earth:

1. **The Village** — A vibrant oasis where the story begins. Meet locals, gather clues, prepare.
2. **Abu Fanous Cave** — The first trial. Earn the lantern and learn to interact with spirits.
3. **Umm Al-Sabyan Cave** — A perilous descent with trickier puzzles and more dangerous jinn.
4. **The Final Dungeon** — A monumental structure where the truth is revealed and the ultimate challenge awaits.

---

## 🏮 Core Mechanic — The Magical Lantern

The lantern is your most important tool:

- **Illumination** — Pierce the absolute darkness of ancient caves and reveal the path forward.
- **Spirit Sight** — Expose hidden paths, invisible enemies, and spectral clues left by the jinn.
- **Capture & Release** — Trap hostile spirits and solve light-based puzzles; captured energy unlocks ancient mechanisms.

## ⚔️ Combat

- **Swordplay** — Precise, fast-paced melee against physical and spectral foes.
- **Wand Magic** — Ancient Nabataean sorcery to strike from range and exploit enemy weaknesses.
- **Tactical Dodging** — Learn enemy patterns and stay one step ahead of the jinn.

## 👥 Characters

All characters and creatures are rooted in genuine Arabian folklore.

| Character | Role |
|---|---|
| **Oday (عدّي)** | The protagonist. Fights with his grandfather's sword, said in legend to bind demons. |
| **Taym (تيّم)** | Oday's older brother. Loves exploration, driven by loyalty, but lacks combat skill. |
| **Abu Fanous (أبو فانوس)** | A legendary jinn cloaked in black with only his lantern visible — he leads lost travelers further astray. |
| **Umm Al-Subyan (أم الصبيان)** | A legendary jinn who takes the form of a woman to lure in the unwary. |

## 🎨 Art Direction

The visual identity blends nostalgic retro pixel art with the environmental storytelling of modern adventure games. Characters are drawn in an authentic ancient Arabian-Nabataean style, and the jinn are designed directly from traditional folklore.

**Palette** — inspired by Nabataean pottery, desert flora, and the AlUla landscape at dawn and dusk:

`Sandstone` · `Gold` · `Earth` · `Night Sky`
